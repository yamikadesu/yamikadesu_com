Commissions

Simple Song (1-3 instruments) 50€ per minute
Moderate Song (4-7 instruments) 70€ per minute
Complex Song (+7 instruments) 100€ per minute

The prices above are estimates. The price will be proportional to the duration of the song.

How I work

I usually show previews of my work, following this order:1. Initial planning for the melody with just the piano
2. Add the main loops, drops and melodies of the main parts
3. Add some improvements that you propose
4. Final review for minor changes

Terms of Service

  • You can freely use the songs always that you tag me. I post my commissions on my Youtube Channel. If you want keep your commission out of my channel, the price would increase by 50% of the base price, but my name must remain. You can freely choose the name of the song.

  • The commission process will last approximately 1 or 2 weeks for each minute requested, also depending on the number of instruments used. You can request a higher speed but the price would increase by 100% of the base price.

  • All the songs will keep as instrumental, if you want more editing or adding vocals I recommend doing them separately, but we can also negotiate.
